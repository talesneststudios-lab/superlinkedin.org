Path-aware two-host routing: www = marketing, app = dashboard

- server.js: replace single-canonical-host middleware with a path-aware one. APP_PATH_PREFIXES (/app, /auth, /api, /checkout, /billing, /onboarding, /upgrade, /playbook, /logout) are 301-redirected to APP_HOST; everything else (landing, marketing pages) is redirected to MARKETING_HOST. Apex (superlinkedin.org) is also routed to the appropriate subdomain.
- server.js: introduce APP_HOST / MARKETING_HOST / APP_BASE / MARKETING_BASE constants (env-overridable) and use them for referral and invite links so referrals land on www and invites land on app.
- .env.example: document the two-host setup with APP_HOST, MARKETING_HOST, and COOKIE_DOMAIN=.superlinkedin.org so sessions span both subdomains.

Pairs with App Runner having both www and app as Active custom domains.
