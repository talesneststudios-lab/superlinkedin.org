Switch LinkedIn Account

Sign in with a different LinkedIn account. Only one LinkedIn can post from this workspace at a time — the new account will replace the current one.

⚠ Heads up

The LinkedIn currently linked to this workspace will be unlinked. Its queue and history stay in our database, but it won't be able to post until you sign back in as that account. The new account will start fresh through onboarding.

ⓘ Before you connect
  1. Open linkedin.com in a new tab and sign out of your current account
  2. Come back here and click "Connect with LinkedIn" below
  3. Sign in with the different LinkedIn account you want to switch to

LinkedIn will authenticate whichever account is currently signed in there. Sign out first if it's the wrong one.

Before you use Engage

Engage helps you draft and post comments and replies using your connected LinkedIn account. By using Discover, Mentions, Replies, or Auto-Engage, you agree to the following:

!

Spammy, automated, or obviously inauthentic activity can lead to action on your LinkedIn account under LinkedIn’s Professional Community Policies and automated-abuse rules. You are solely responsible for what you post.

  • All replies must be genuine, relevant, and written in your own voice.
  • AI-generated suggestions must be reviewed and meaningfully edited before posting. Do not post AI output verbatim, especially across many threads.
  • You will not use unauthorized scripts, bots, or third-party automation alongside Engage in ways that violate LinkedIn’s terms.
  • Reply activity may be logged or reviewed. SuperLinkedIn may suspend accounts that show patterns inconsistent with these rules or LinkedIn’s policies.
  • Daily and plan-based limits apply to replies and automated actions.

Before you send DMs

This feature sends or queues messages using your LinkedIn account. Bulk messages are delivered through SuperLinkedIn’s paced sender — please review the points below:

!

Unwanted or spammy outreach may result in restriction or suspension of your LinkedIn account under LinkedIn’s policies. You alone are responsible for every message.

  • Bulk DM sends from SuperLinkedIn are spaced about 1–2 minutes apart to reduce rate-limit risk.
  • Delivery respects LinkedIn’s rules and each member’s messaging preferences (connections, Message requests, Premium features, Open Profile/InMail).
  • For prospecting outreach, only message people where you have a lawful basis — for example documented consent or a clear expectation of relevance (and never purchased lists scraped without permission).
  • DM and Bulk DM usage counts toward your plan quotas and documented daily/monthly caps.
