How to grow on LinkedIn without gimmicks

If you typed how to grow on LinkedIn, you’re likely balancing pipeline pressure with creative burnout. Sustainable growth stacks three layers: repeatable creative hypotheses, humane engagement, and instrumentation so you prune losers fast.

1. Commit to one wedge for ninety days

Audience algorithms stabilise faster when lexical signals stay coherent: same buyer, geography, toolkit, worldview. Scattershot expertise confuses embeddings and humans alike.

2. Run format experiments sequentially

Test carousel depth vs text-only essays vs clipped video—but only one novelty per fortnight—else attribution muddies.

3. Engine comments like product research

Reply-depth signals outperform vanity likes. Scripts help: acknowledge, extend, ask a sharpening question.

4. Reflect weekly with numbers, not vibes

Inspect saves, follower velocity, qualitative DM quality. Decide what earned another episode next week.
