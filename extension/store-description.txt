SuperLinkedIn – LinkedIn Analytics & AI Growth Tools

Super insights for your LinkedIn activities and engagements

SuperLinkedIn is the most complete Chrome extension for LinkedIn power-users who want to grow faster, post smarter and analyse deeper – without leaving the feed.

• LinkedIn analytics extension • LinkedIn analytics dashboard • post scheduler • AI post writer • follower tracker • social media analytics • LinkedIn growth tool • post inspiration • productivity tools

━━━━━━━━
❶ WHY SUPERLINKEDIN

If you ever searched for "LinkedIn analytics", "Post scheduler", "how to grow on LinkedIn" or "AI post writer", SuperLinkedIn covers them all in one lightweight sidebar. Install, pin it once and you unlock real-time insights every time you open linkedin.com.

━━━━━━━━
❷ CORE ANALYTICS

Overview graphs – activity frequency, impressions, engagement rates.
Best posts & worst posts – identify what really works.
Media vs text performance – compare photo, link, and hashtag reach.
Follower growth tracker – daily, weekly, monthly change.
Engagement breakdown – see every like, comment & repost across your content.
Profile interactions – instant stats when visiting any LinkedIn profile.

━━━━━━━━
❸ AI-POWERED TOOLKIT

Generate Post – one-click AI post generation for any topic.
Improve Writing – rephrase drafts in your tone of voice.
Quick Improve – Grammar, Translate, Hook, Concise, Engaging, Humorous modes.
Post Inspiration – discover proven hooks, CTAs & ideas from viral posts.
Context-aware – uses your onboarding preferences, favorite creators, and products.
