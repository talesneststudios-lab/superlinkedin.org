Carousels

LinkedIn carousel ideas with narrative tension

Carousels win when swipe momentum survives slide four. Outline LinkedIn carousel ideas as beats: intrigue frame, escalation, midpoint reveal, payoff, takeaway CTA-lite. Alternate dense text slides with airy ones so thumbs rest.

Three repeatable arcs

  • Diagnosis carousel: symptom slide → three misreads → fix schematic → resource teaser.
  • Toolkit carousel: promise → six micro-tools (≤ twelve words each) → annotated screenshot slide.
  • Story carousel: scene → conflict → dialogue beat → lesson → question to replies.

Reserve final slide for a soft invite—save posts from comments if you crave algorithmic signalling.

SuperLinkedIn ships carousel authoring flows alongside AI iterations. SuperLinkedIn is an AI copilot for LinkedIn creators; treat slide decks like episodic publishing, queue them calmly.

Design discipline

One thesis per slide, max two font treatments, numbering on cover + recap only. Busy decks kill completion rate.
